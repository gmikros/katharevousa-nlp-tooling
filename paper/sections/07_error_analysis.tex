\section{Error Analysis and Optimization Path}

Several optimization passes were informative even when they did not improve final metrics:
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.25em]
  \item learning-rate schedule and loss reweighting changes often caused broad regressions,
  \item hard-case augmentation did not consistently improve generalization,
  \item tokenizer truncation alignment required strict old/new index mapping to avoid head-index errors,
  \item framework-level strictness in Stanza exposed latent structural inconsistencies in portions of the data.
\end{itemize}

This history is important for reproducibility: negative results narrowed the viable region and justified selecting \texttt{v1\_opt} as the release candidate.
